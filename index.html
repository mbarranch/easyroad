Services.

We Buy Cars

We conduct professional inspections on vehicles, and provide objective and fair purchase prices based on relevant databases, ensuring that the price truly reflects the value of the car. On-site evaluations and purchases can be scheduled.

We Sell Cars

The vehicles sold come with a CARFAX report and an Inspection Report, and include a 30-day warranty service for three major systems. Prices are clearly marked, with no hidden fees.

We Rent Cars

We offer a variety of rental vehicles including sedans, vans, and SUVs, available for both short-term and long-term rentals at fair and economical prices. The rental vehicles are regularly inspected and maintained, cleaned and prepared, ensuring peace of mind for users.

And Much More!

Our services include convenient trade-in options for vehicle upgrades with efficient and accurate valuation, alongside streamlined financial services in collaboration with banks and financial institutions, designed to cater to the unique financial requirements of our customers.
